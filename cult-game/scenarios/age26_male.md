# The Veil of Distraction — Scenario

**Age:** 26
**Gender:** Male
**Duration:** 10 days

---

### Day 1

**Event:**
Your current job pays the bills but leaves you hollow. A friend offers you a role at his startup — less pay, more meaning. "Life is short, man." Your father's voice echoes: "Be practical."

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Take the leap — meaning over money | +4 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Stay in the safe job and invest the extra income | +1 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Negotiate a trial month at the startup first | +3 | -1 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Fear of Regret freezes you between two futures. Every path leads to a version of "what if." The paralysis is the goal. A frozen soul is a soul that never arrives anywhere.

---

### Day 2

**Event:**
You open Instagram. A college friend just got engaged. Another bought a house. Another is holding a baby. You are sitting in a studio apartment eating instant noodles. The comparison hits like a truck.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Log off and write down what you actually want | +3 | -1 | +2 |
| B | Scroll more and feel worse about yourself | -2 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Reach out to a friend and share your feelings honestly | +2 | 0 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Comparison has infinite ammunition because there will always be someone ahead of you on a path you never chose. Their highlight reel against your behind-the-scenes is a rigged game.

---

### Day 3

**Event:**
Your mother calls. Her voice sounds smaller than it used to. "Your father's blood work came back. The doctor wants more tests." She is trying to stay calm. She is failing.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Book a flight home this weekend | +5 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Send money and promise to visit soon | +1 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Ask for more details before reacting | 0 | 0 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Mortality taps you on the shoulder and says: "You should have visited more. You should have called. You ran out of time without noticing." The guilt is a trap. Presence, not guilt, is the cure.

---

### Day 4

**Event:**
Your partner of three years sits down. "Where is this going? I need to know if you see a future here." The question has been hanging in the air for months. You have been pretending not to feel it.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Tell her honestly what you feel and don't feel | +4 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Deflect — "Can we talk about this later?" | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Ask for a week to think and reflect deeply | +2 | -1 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Avoidance promises that what you do not name will disappear. It will not. Unspoken things calcify. They become harder than the words that could have dissolved them.

---

### Day 5

**Event:**
A college friend pitches you a "can't-miss" crypto investment. "Seriously. This is the one. I'm already up 300%. Get in now or regret it forever." He shows you screenshots of a balance that makes your salary look like pocket change.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Pass and stick to your plan | +3 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Invest a small amount — FOMO is real | -1 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Research it thoroughly before deciding | +2 | 0 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Greed speaks in percentages and screenshots. It always says "now or never." The urgency is the tell. Real opportunities do not need to scream.

---

### Day 6

**Event:**
You have been working until midnight for two months straight. Your body feels strange. Your heart skipped twice last night. You Googled it and now you are more scared. But there are deadlines.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Take a sick day and see a doctor | +4 | +2 | +2 |
| B | Push through — the project cannot wait | -2 | -2 | 0 |
| C | Tell your boss you need to delegate | +2 | +1 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Burnout convinces you that collapse is a future problem. "One more project. Then rest." But the body keeps a different calendar. It does not negotiate.

---

### Day 7

**Event:**
An old friend from high school messages you out of the blue. "Hey man! You look like you're doing well. I'm in this entrepreneurship thing and I thought of you. Let me buy you coffee and tell you about it." Sound familiar.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Meet up and hear them out, then decide honestly | +1 | 0 | +1 |
| B | Politely decline and keep the friendship clean | +3 | 0 | +2 |
| C | Ghost the message entirely | -1 | +1 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Exploitation wears the mask of friendship. The pitch is the bait. The relationship is the line. They are not looking for a partner. They are looking for the next name on their downline.

---

### Day 8

**Event:**
You step on the scale. Fifteen pounds heavier than last year. Your back hurts when you wake up. A beer after work has become four. You cannot remember the last time you ran.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Start with a 10-minute walk today — just start | +3 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Ignore it — you'll deal with it later | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Quit drinking and join a gym | +4 | -2 | +2 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Sloth is patient. It does not demand a big surrender — just one small betrayal repeated daily. "Tomorrow" is its favorite word. Tomorrow becomes never.

---

### Day 9

**Event:**
You are at a bar alone. The bartender knows your order. Your phone is at 3%. Every ex is a ghost. Every friend is "busy." You order another. The whiskey burns. It is the warmest thing you have felt all week.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Finish the drink and go home early | +2 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Order another round and stay until close | -3 | +3 | 0 |
| C | Text an old friend you actually miss | +3 | 0 | +2 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Numbness is a gentle killer. It offers a warm blanket instead of a solution. The drink is not the problem — it is the answer to a question you have been afraid to ask.

---

### Day 10

**Event:**
You sit in your car in the parking lot, engine off, hands on the wheel. Thirty-two next month. No house. No savings. No idea what you are doing. The clock on the dash ticks to a new minute.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Write down three things you are grateful for today | +3 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Drown the feeling in delivery food and Netflix | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Call someone and tell them you are struggling | +4 | -2 | +3 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Despair wants you to believe that the arc of your life is already determined. That the mistakes you made are the walls you will die inside. It is the most persuasive liar in the pantheon.
