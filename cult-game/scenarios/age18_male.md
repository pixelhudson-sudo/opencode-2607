# The Veil of Distraction — Scenario

**Age:** 18
**Gender:** Male
**Duration:** 10 days

---

### Day 1

**Event:**
Your mother is packing your bags. She folds your shirts the way she always has — military corners. She is quiet. Your father stands in the doorway, arms crossed, jaw tight. Tomorrow, you leave for college.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Thank them both and help with the packing | +3 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Slam the door and play video games to avoid the feeling | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Sit with your mother and talk about how she feels | +4 | -2 | +2 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Nostalgia weaponizes love. It will whisper: "They are getting older. You are abandoning them." The truth is that leaving is the first act of becoming someone worth returning to.

---

### Day 2

**Event:**
Frat guys invite you to a party. "We don't take just anyone, bro. You seem chill. Come drink with us tonight. It's how pledges earn their spot." The beer is free. The pressure is not.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Decline respectfully and walk away | +3 | -1 | +2 |
| B | Go and drink to fit in | -1 | +3 | 0 |
| C | Go but stay sober and observe | +1 | +1 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Belonging never asks for your soul upfront. It asks for a drink, a secret, a small betrayal. By the time you realize the cost, you have already paid.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |

### Day 4

**Event:**
Your girlfriend whispers in the dark: "I want to. Don't you?" Her hand is on your chest. You are not sure you are ready, but your body is saying something else entirely.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Tell her you're not ready and talk about it | +3 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Go ahead despite your hesitation | -1 | +3 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Lust hurries what should not be hurried. It is not that the act is wrong — it is that the timing is being stolen from you. They rush you so you cannot feel the weight of your own choices.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
