# The Veil of Distraction — Scenario

**Age:** 26
**Gender:** Female
**Duration:** 10 days

---

### Day 1

**Event:**
Your aunt at the family dinner: "So... any special someone? You're not getting younger, you know. Your cousin just had her second." She smiles sweetly. The whole table turns.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | "I'm focusing on myself right now" — calm and firm | +2 | 0 | +1 |
| B | Snap at her to mind her own business | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Excuse yourself and cry in the bathroom | -1 | -1 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Clock ticks inside you now, installed by a thousand comments like this one. It measures your worth in milestones. The loudest lie is that a fertile womb is the sum of a woman's value.

---

### Day 2

**Event:**
You got the promotion. It requires relocating. Your partner says: "I support you, but I can't leave my job. We'd be long-distance." The offer is a year. A year is a long time when you do not know what survives.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Take the promotion and trust the relationship | +3 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Decline the promotion to stay together | +1 | 0 | 0 |
| C | Have a real conversation about what each of you needs | +4 | -2 | +3 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Trade-off frames love and ambition as enemies. They are not. But the middle path requires conversations most people never learn to have. The silence is where resentment grows.

---

### Day 3

**Event:**
You open Instagram. A friend's engagement photos. Another friend's wedding. Another's baby announcement. You are happy for them. But there is something else in your chest too — a low hum you try to ignore.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Log off and take yourself on a solo date | +3 | 0 | +1 |
| B | Scroll more and spiral into self-pity | -2 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Text one of them and genuinely celebrate them | +4 | -1 | +2 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Envy does not need you to resent them. It just needs you to doubt yourself. The comparison is not about their joy — it is about the gap you perceive between where you are and where you were told you should be.

---

### Day 4

**Event:**
Your partner said something that felt wrong. "You're too sensitive. It was a joke." But it did not feel like a joke. You keep replaying it. Maybe you ARE too sensitive. Maybe that is exactly what he wants you to think.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | "My feelings are not up for debate" — hold your ground | +5 | -2 | +3 |
| B | Apologize and doubt yourself | -2 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Write down exactly what happened while it is fresh | +3 | -1 | +2 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Gaslight teaches a terrible language: "You are misremembering. You are overreacting. That did not happen." The mouth of the demon wears a familiar face. Trust your perception. It is the only compass you have.

---

### Day 5

**Event:**

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Invisibility is the daily bread of systemic bias. It does not always shout. Sometimes it is just a name being said instead of yours. But the sum of a thousand erasures is an erased life.

### Day 6

**Options:**

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |

| B | Delete the email from their inbox if you can and pray | -2 | +1 | 0 |
