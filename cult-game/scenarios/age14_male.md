# The Veil of Distraction — Scenario

**Age:** 14
**Gender:** Male
**Duration:** 10 days

---

### Day 1

**Event:**
Your mother stands at your door, arms crossed. "I asked you three times already to clean your room." Her voice has that tired edge — the one that means she has been holding it in all day.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Nod and start cleaning without a word | +3 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Scream "I'll do it later!" and slam the door | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Mumble an excuse and keep gaming | -1 | +1 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> This fight has occurred 47 times this year. The demon Resentment feeds on the loop: anger, silence, guilt, repeat. The room is never the point. The pattern is always the point.

---

### Day 2

**Event:**
At lunch, your friend leans in. "We're gonna skip fourth period. Mr. Henderson's sub. You in?" Three other kids are watching, waiting for your answer.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Say no and walk to class | +3 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Join them, heart racing | -1 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Tell the teacher you're worried about them | +2 | -2 | +2 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Belonging whispers: "They will accept you if you break the rules with them." It was the same voice that made the first human eat the fruit. The fruit is always an apple of rejection.

### Day 3

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |

### Day 5

**Event:**
Two friends are fighting. One pulls you aside. "It's me or her. Choose." You had no idea this was coming. Your stomach drops.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|

### Day 6

### Day 10

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Block and report them immediately | +3 | 0 | +2 |
| B | Reply with fake info out of curiosity | -2 | +1 | 0 |
