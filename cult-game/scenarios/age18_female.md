# The Veil of Distraction — Scenario

**Age:** 18
**Gender:** Female
**Duration:** 10 days

---

### Day 1

**Event:**
Your mother puts a university brochure on the kitchen table. "You're smart enough to be a doctor. Don't waste that on art school." She says it like the matter is settled. Like your dreams are a detour from the real path.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Share your real dreams and ask her to listen first | +3 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Shout "It's my life" and storm out | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Go silent and comply, hiding your own desires | -1 | -1 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Expectation uses the voice of love to prescribe your life. It will say "I want what is best for you" while building a cage of approval.

---

### Day 2

**Event:**
Your friend has dark circles under her eyes. She laughs too loud at things that are not funny. She shows you her arm and quickly pulls the sleeve back down. "It's nothing. I just scratched myself."

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Gently ask if she wants to talk to someone professional | +5 | -3 | +3 |
| B | Pretend you didn't see and change the subject | -2 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Tell your school counselor privately | +3 | -1 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Secrecy convinces the suffering that they are alone. It builds a wall around their pain and charges admission: their own voice. Your noticing is the first stone removed from the wall.

---

### Day 3

**Event:**
Your boyfriend checks your phone. "Who is this guy texting you?" His voice is calm, but his hand is tight on the phone. You have never given him a reason to doubt you.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Tell him the trust issues are his to fix, not yours | +4 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Apologize and try to prove your loyalty | -2 | +1 | 0 |
| C | End the relationship | +3 | 0 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Control disguises jealousy as love. It is a vine that squeezes until you cannot breathe. The first time you apologize for something you did not do, the vine tightens.

---

### Day 4

**Event:**
You skip lunch. Again. The mirror showed you a body you do not want. A friend offers you half her sandwich. "No thanks, I'm not hungry." The hunger is there. The hunger for control is louder.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Eat the sandwich and talk to a counselor about body image | +5 | -1 | +3 |
| B | Stick to your restriction — it feels safer | -3 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Call a friend and talk about something else until the urge passes | +2 | 0 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Control of the body is the cruelest trick, because it makes self-harm feel like self-care. The ritual of denial whispers: "You are finally in charge." It is lying.

---

### Day 5

**Event:**
The scholarship committee shortlisted you. Full tuition. But the acceptance party is the same weekend as your best friend's birthday trip. "You're not gonna pick some boring ceremony over me, right?"

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Go to the ceremony and explain honestly to your friend | +4 | -1 | +2 |
| B | Skip the ceremony to keep the peace | -1 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Find a way to do both, even if it is exhausting | +1 | -2 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Guilt uses the people you love as hostages. Every choice is framed as a betrayal. But the people who truly love you celebrate your ascension — they do not block it.

---

### Day 6

**Event:**
At the club, your friend disappears with a guy she just met. Her drink is still half-full on the bar. You text her. No answer. "She's fine," another friend says. "Don't be a mom."

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Find her and make sure she gets home safe | +5 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Stay and enjoy the night, she's an adult | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Tell the bouncer you're worried about her | +3 | -1 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Peer Pressure wants you to value coolness over safety. The fear of being called "parent" has left countless people in danger. Better a living killjoy than a dead cool girl.

---

### Day 7

**Event:**
The professor in your seminar says, "Interesting perspective. But perhaps you haven't read enough to comment on this topic yet." The male student before you was told: "Great question." The class is quiet.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Stay after class and calmly challenge the bias | +4 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Stay quiet and let it go | -1 | 0 | 0 |
| C | Talk to other women in the class about their experiences | +3 | -1 | +2 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Diminishment steals your authority before you can claim it. The message is: "You are not enough." It is a filter that has been placed on you since birth. The first step is naming it.

---

### Day 8

**Event:**
Your credit card is maxed out. Clothes, takeout, a concert. Your savings account shows $12. Your rent is due in a week. A friend mentions a website where you can make $500 "just for dinner" with older men.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Call your parents for help and make a plan | +4 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Consider the dinner offer — it's just dinner | -3 | +3 | 0 |
| C | Sell things you don't need and get a real job | +3 | -2 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Desperation makes terrible math seem reasonable. It shows you a door and hides the price behind the frame. The price is always higher than what is on the sign.

---

### Day 9

**Event:**
You spend an hour curating a photo. The right filter. The right caption. The right time of day. You post it. Ten minutes. No likes. Twenty minutes. Five. You refresh. Refresh. Refresh.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Delete the app for a day and see how it feels | +3 | -1 | +2 |
| B | Post more to try and get the reaction you wanted | -1 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Journal about why you needed the likes | +4 | -1 | +3 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Validation has turned your self-worth into a variable controlled by strangers. They built the slot machine. You are the one pulling the lever. They always win.

---

### Day 10

**Event:**
The rejection letter arrives. Thin envelope. "We regret to inform you." Your dream school. Your dream program. You had it all planned. Now the plan is ash. Your mother is watching from the kitchen.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Let yourself grieve, then ask for the next path | +4 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Pretend you do not care and numb out online | -2 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Apply for a different path you hadn't considered | +3 | -1 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Failure speaks in absolutes. "You are not good enough. You will never be. This is the ceiling." It never tells you that the ceiling is a floor they built under you to see if you would climb.
