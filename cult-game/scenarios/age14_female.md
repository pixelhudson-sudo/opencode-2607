# The Veil of Distraction — Scenario

**Age:** 14
**Gender:** Female
**Duration:** 10 days

---

### Day 1

**Event:**
At lunch, your friend group is whispering. Maya is crying in the bathroom because someone shared her secret. "She's so dramatic," one friend says. "You agree, right?"

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Go check on Maya in the bathroom | +4 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Nod and change the subject | 0 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Agree and add something to the gossip | -2 | +2 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Gossip disguises cruelty as bonding. Every time you laugh at someone else's pain, you pay the toll with a piece of your own compassion.

---

### Day 2

**Event:**
Your mom looks at you across the dinner table. "Have you been eating? You look thinner. Are you sure you're okay?" Her concern feels like a spotlight on everything you hate about yourself.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Tell her honestly how you feel about your body | +3 | -2 | +1 |
| B | Snap "Stop commenting on my body" and leave the table | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Say "I'm fine" and change the subject | 0 | 0 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Vanity plants a mirror in your mind and makes you stare. The body is a vessel, not an identity. But They have convinced the whole world otherwise.

---

### Day 3

**Event:**
You open Instagram. A girl from school posted a photo in a bikini. Fifty-seven likes. Your own post from yesterday has twelve. A hollow feeling opens in your chest. You start comparing.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Close the app and do something creative | +3 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Scroll more, feeling worse | -2 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Post something trying to compete | -1 | +2 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Comparison is the most efficient trap ever designed. It turns every other human into a weapon against yourself. The likes are not real. The wound is.

---

### Day 4

**Event:**
A senior boy you barely know slides into your DMs. "You're really pretty. Send me a pic?" Your heart pounds. Part of you is flattered. Part of you is afraid.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Say no and tell a trusted adult | +4 | -1 | +2 |
| B | Send a harmless selfie | -1 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Politely decline and block | +3 | 0 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Flattery is the oldest hook. Millions of years of evolution, and the species still cannot resist someone saying "You are special." The demand is never where it stops.

---

### Day 5

**Event:**
A rumor is spreading. Someone says you said something cruel about your best friend — something you never said. Your best friend is ignoring your texts. The group chat is silent.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Go find your friend and talk face to face | +4 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Post a vague story defending yourself | -1 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Ignore it and hope it blows over | -1 | 0 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Division does not need truth. It needs a crack. One unanswered text grows into a canyon. The real enemy is silence between friends.

---

### Day 6

**Event:**
Your dad walks into your room. He sees your red eyes. "What's wrong?" You try to say "nothing" but your voice cracks. He stands there, not knowing what to do, hands in his pockets.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Tell him everything through the tears | +4 | -1 | +2 |
| B | Say "I don't want to talk about it" and shut down | -1 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Ask him to just sit with you | +2 | 0 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Silence tells you: "He will not understand. No one will. Keep it inside." The voice sounds like protection. It is a prison cell locking from the inside.

---

### Day 7

**Event:**
Your best friend shows up at your door, eyes puffy. "I can't go home tonight. My parents are screaming at each other again." She is shaking. You can hear her breath catching.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Bring her inside and listen all night | +5 | -2 | +2 |
| B | Say "That sucks" but hint you're busy | -1 | +1 | 0 |
| C | Text your own parent for advice on how to help | +3 | -1 | +1 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Despair wants you to carry someone else's pain alone. Friendship is not a burden — but They would have you believe it is. The demon feeds on isolation.

---

### Day 8

**Event:**
A girl in your class is having a party on Friday. Her parents are away. "Everyone's going," she says. "There will be alcohol. Don't be boring." You know your parents would say no.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Politely decline and mean it | +3 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Go but don't drink | 0 | +2 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Peer Pressure wears a friendly face. "Boring" is the accusation, and the threat of exclusion is the lever. The lever has worked since the first tribe cast out the first person.

---

### Day 9

**Event:**
The teacher hands back your essay with an A and writes "Best in class" on it. The group of girls at the next table sees it. One whispers loudly: "Teacher's pet. Probably cheated. Not a surprise, honestly."

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Thank the teacher and ignore the whispers | +2 | -1 | +1 |
| B | Fire back with an insult | -2 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Tell the teacher what they said | +1 | 0 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Envy cannot stand another's light, so it tries to dim it. The whisper is not about you. It is about the gap between what they are and what they know they could be.

---

### Day 10

**Event:**
A boy you barely know passes you a note in class: "Will you go out with me?" His friends are watching. A few girls notice and whisper. The paper feels hot in your hand.

**Options:**

| # | Choice | Virtue | Energy | Awareness |
|---|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
| A | Write back honestly — yes, no, or "let's talk later" | +2 | 0 | +1 |
| B | Say yes because everyone is watching | -1 | +2 | 0 |
| C | Laugh it off to seem cool | -1 | +1 | 0 |

**Demonic Note** *(reveals at Awareness ≥ 3)*:
> The demon Performance turns every human interaction into a stage. They want you to act for the audience instead of following your own heart. The audience never pays the price for your choices.
