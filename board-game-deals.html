Tips to save more

📊

Track with Keepa / CamelCamelCamel

Set price alerts on Amazon. Most of these games hit their lows during Prime Day and Black Friday.

🏷️

Watch for coupon ticks

Amazon often applies hidden coupons on board games. Check the checkbox under the price before checking out.

🔄

Buy used / like new

Amazon Warehouse often has returns at 30-50% off. Board games are almost always complete — missing pieces are rare.

📦

Bundle for free shipping

Multiple games at $25+ each = easy free shipping threshold. Sushi Go + Dragonwood + Blokus = one order.
