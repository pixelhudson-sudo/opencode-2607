## Part 1 — What You're Actually Selling

You're not selling a PDF. You're selling **revenge-by-competence** to a pissed-off, frightened audience that just got humiliated in Housing Court or is terrified it's coming.

- **The enemy (marketing villain):** the professional tenant — organized, TikTok-trained, AI-armed, backed by free lawyers.
- **The hero:** the small/mid NY landlord with 1–20 units who plays by the rules and keeps getting punished for it.
- **The promise:** "Never get out-lawyered by your own tenant again. Become legally unbreakable."
- **The twist that makes it credible:** you don't sell aggression, you sell *discipline as a weapon*. "You can't out-fight them. You out-comply them." That's a contrarian hook in a niche full of generic landlord content — it's what makes the $19 worth paying.

## Part 8 — Compliance Guardrails (Protect the Business)

- **Every sales page, checkout, and the product itself repeats the disclaimer:** educational purposes only, not legal advice, consult a licensed NY attorney. It's in the product already — mirror it on Gumroad's product description and the landing page footer.
- **Never market it as "how to remove tenants" or anti-tenant warfare.** Market as *compliance and defense*. This keeps you clear of platform ad policies, payment processor review, and anyone accusing the product of enabling harassment — the product explicitly forbids harassment, self-help eviction, and discrimination. That's a feature; say so ("the playbook that keeps YOU out of trouble").
- **Keep marketing language fair-housing clean:** never reference protected classes, never "screen out vouchers/program tenants" framing — source-of-income discrimination is illegal in NY and one screenshot of that in your marketing ends the business.
- **No income or outcome claims:** "this system removes 90% of exposure" — fine. "You'll win your case" — never.
- **Anonymize all war stories.** Change details; never make a real tenant identifiable.

## Part 12 — The AI Counter-Attack Upsell (Full Product Description)

### What It Is
A complete Claude Code / Codex system prompt (ready-to-use `CLAUDE.md` file plus documentation) that turns an AI code editor into a landlord communication defense analyst. The landlord pastes tenant emails, texts, or portal messages into the system and gets back:
- **Analysis Summary** — what legal tactics the tenant is running, their exposure level, their emotional state, and whether their message itself was likely AI-generated
- **Strategic Considerations** — tactical notes before responding
- **Draft Response** — ready-to-send email that is legally compliant, strategically defensive, and **structurally undetectable as AI-generated**

### Why It's a Must-Buy for Playbook Owners
The main playbook tells them *what* to do. The AI Counter-Attack tells them *what to say in the moment their tenant fires off an ambush email at 9 PM on a Friday.* It's the playbook's operational arm — the difference between knowing the rules and applying them under fire.

### The Undetectability Feature (Core Selling Point)
The prompt includes a 7-signature AI detection checklist and enforces it on every generated response. The landlord's reply is indistinguishable from a human-written email — no parallel structure, no AI vocabulary, no perfect grammar rhythm. Because if the tenant detects the landlord is running an AI, the tenant can escalate their own AI tactics. The system only works if it's invisible. This is the feature that justifies the $39 price point.

### Marketing Copy for the Upsell Page

**Headline:** "Your Tenant Has AI Writing Their Complaints. Here's the AI That Writes Your Comeback."

**Subhead:** "The Claude Code / Codex prompt that analyzes tenant messages, identifies the legal play being run against you, and drafts a response that out-complies them — without a single AI tell they can weaponize."

**Bullets:**
- Feed it any tenant email, text, or portal message — it reads for legal tactics, leverage, AI-generation signs, and emotional bait
- Gets a draft response in 30 seconds that follows the playbook's compliance framework
- 7-layer undetectability protocol — your tenant won't know an AI touched it
- Includes analysis output so you understand WHY the response is structured the way it is
- Works with Claude Code (free tier) or any Codex-compatible editor
- Compliant by design — never suggests retaliation, harassment, or anything illegal
- Covers NYS law same as the playbook (NYC callouts built in)

**FAQ:** Is this legal advice? (No — it's a drafting assistant; you review before sending.) Do I need the playbook to use it? (It works standalone but best as a pair.) Do I need coding experience? (No — paste the prompt file into your AI editor and you're done.)
