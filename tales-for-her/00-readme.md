# Tales for Her

A story engine for your daughter.

Every child deserves stories built just for them — ones that carry your voice, your values, your family's secret history. These stories are how she'll know who she is and who she can become.

## How It Works

1. **Fill in the input files**
   - `01-values.md` — the virtues you're planting
   - `02-story-types.md` — what she loves
   - `03-family-background.md` — the real stuff, woven in

2. **Tell me you're done**

3. **I'll write the story** — a complete, original tale built from your inputs, with her as the heroine, your ancestors as her guides, and your values as the lessons she lives.

## After That

You can ask for another one anytime. They'll all live in this folder — a growing library of *her* stories, from *you*.

---

📖 **Tip:** Fill these out however feels good. One sentence is enough. A paragraph is better. Skip what you don't know. Invent what you wish were true.
